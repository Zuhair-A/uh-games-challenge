About

UH Centennial Games & Apps is a dynamic innovation challenge designed to celebrate creativity, technology, and collaboration among developers, designers, and storytellers. Participants will design games or applications that embody the Centennial spirit — blending heritage, innovation, and impact for the next 100 years.
This event is open to students, professionals, and enthusiasts who are passionate about shaping the future of interactive experiences.
The UH Centennial Games invites students to design and ship AI‑powered games or applications that improve life at UH and across Houston — from graduation success and belonging to wellness and sustainability. Finalists will demo live; winners will be showcased university‑wide.
